The SPEII employs a non-parametric statistical estimate of the undercount of founders. It estimates a distribution over the likely number of hidden LGBTQ+ entrepreneurs in each Metro. This distribution is derived from three variables: $$ \begin{aligned} \left( pop_{\text{lgbtq}} \right )_{i} &= \text{Metro's local LGBTQ+ population} \\ \left( e_{\text{rate}} \right )_{i} &= \text{Metro's entrepreneurship rate} \\ \left( e_{\text{lgbtq}_{\text{mod}}} \right )_{i} &= \text{correction factor for LGBTQ+ entrepreneurship rates} \\ \end{aligned} $$
